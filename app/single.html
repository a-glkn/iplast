Various options for the walls, handles and bottom of containers allow us to satisfy the needs of the most demanding customers.  Economical, hygienic, with well-ventilated perforated walls, containers are perfect for storage and transportation vegetables and fruits from the moment of harvesting to its sale in retail stores.

Containers are adapted for automated warehouse systems and conveyors. Versions of containers with reinforced bottoms allow them to withstand the load up to 50 kg and be effective in various industrial sectors. Each container can be equipped with an individual lid. Additional components allow you to fix the lid on the container.

The EC line of Euro containers is compatible with the universal containers of the I-Plast line, allowing them to be stacked on top of each other and stacked compact set with dimensions 1200x800 mm for use on a Euro pallet.
